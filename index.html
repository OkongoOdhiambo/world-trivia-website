Did You Know?

The Amazon Rainforest produces 20% of the world's oxygen and is home to about 10% of all known species.

The Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest living structure, visible from space, stretching over 2,300 kilometers.

The Sahara Desert is almost the same size as the entire United States, making it the largest hot desert in the world.

Mount Everest grows about 4 millimeters higher every year due to geological forces pushing the Himalayas upward.
